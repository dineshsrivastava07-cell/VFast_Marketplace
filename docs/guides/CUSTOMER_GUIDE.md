# Customer Guide — VFast

> Quick delivery in 10 minutes. Groceries & essentials at your door.

## 1. Install the app

VFast is an installable PWA — no app store needed.

### On Android (Chrome)
1. Open `https://vfast.co.in` in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu → **Install app** (or "Add to home screen").
3. The "VFast" icon appears on your home screen.

### On iPhone (Safari)
1. Open `https://vfast.co.in` in Safari.
2. Tap **Share** → **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Tap "VFast" on your home screen.

### On Desktop
Chrome / Edge will show an **Install** icon in the address bar — click it.

## 2. Logging in with OTP

VFast is for India only and accepts only Indian (+91) mobile numbers.

1. Tap **Login** in the top right.
2. Enter your **+91 mobile number** (10 digits after +91).
3. Tap **Send OTP** — you'll get a 6-digit code via SMS within seconds (during the demo phase the OTP is also shown on screen).
4. Enter the OTP and tap **Verify**.

You're in. We never ask for a password — every login uses a fresh OTP.

## 3. Set your delivery location

You have two options:
- Tap the location pill (top of the page) and enter your **6-digit PIN code**. We instantly show whether VFast delivers there, your ETA, and the delivery fee.
- *(Coming soon)* tap **Use my GPS** to auto-detect.

If your PIN isn't serviceable yet, leave your contact under **Notify me** — we'll let you know the moment we launch.

## 4. Browsing & searching

- **Six top categories** are shown on the home page. Tap any to dive in.
- Inside a category you'll see **subcategories** as chips along the top, plus filters:
  - Brand
  - Dietary (Veg / Vegan / Non-Veg) — denoted by the Indian green / red dot
  - Price range
  - Minimum discount %
  - In-stock only
- Sort by: relevance, price low→high, price high→low, discount %, or newest.
- Or use the **search bar** at the top — search by product or brand (e.g. "Amul", "Maggi").

Each product card shows:
- Big crisp product photo
- Brand (e.g. "Amul"), name, pack size (e.g. "1 L")
- Selling price + MRP (strike-through) + green discount % chip
- Per-unit price (e.g. "₹2/100g") to compare easily
- Veg/Non-Veg dot (Indian standard)
- ETA badge ("10m") and a low-stock warning ("Only 3 left") where applicable
- **ADD** button (and a stepper once added)

## 5. Cart & checkout

- Tap **ADD** on a product to put it in your cart. Use the stepper to add more.
- Open the cart from the top-right cart pill (desktop) or the sticky cart bar (mobile).
- Inside the cart you see items, prices, and "**You're saving ₹X**".
- Free delivery kicks in above **₹199**.
- Tap **Proceed to checkout**.

On the checkout page:
- Fill the address (Flat, Area, Landmark, City, State, 6-digit PIN, phone).
- Pick a payment method (see §6).
- Tap **Place order**.

## 6. Paying — COD or UPI QR

### Cash on Delivery
Easy: pay the rider in cash when they arrive. No further action.

### UPI QR (Scan & Pay)
1. After placing the order you land on the order page with a **QR code**.
2. Open any UPI app (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, …) and scan the QR.
3. Pay the exact amount shown.
4. Once your UPI app shows **Payment successful**, note down the **UTR / Reference ID** (12–16 digit number) and screenshot the success page.
5. On the order page enter the **UTR** and **upload the screenshot** → tap **Submit proof for verification**.
6. Within ~10 minutes our team verifies the payment and the order moves to "packed". You'll see the status update live.

> If verification fails (typo in UTR, amount mismatch), the order moves to "Payment rejected" and you can retry the payment.

## 7. Tracking your order

The order page shows a live timeline:

```
● Placed  ●  Verifying payment  ●  Packed  ●  Out for delivery  ●  Delivered
```

ETA is shown at the top. Rider's name appears once assigned. The page auto-refreshes every 5 seconds.

## 8. Returns & refund requests

- For damaged / wrong items, tap **Help** on the order page (Phase 3 will add inline buttons) or contact us at `support@vfast.co.in` / `+91 1800-000-000`.
- COD orders: refund is paid by UPI to a number you share — within 24h of approval.
- UPI orders: refund goes back to the same UPI account — within 48h of approval.

## 9. Managing profile and saved addresses

Tap the **person icon** top right:
- **My orders** — every past order, with status and reorder button.
- **Logout** — closes your session on this device.

Multi-address book and account-level preferences are coming in Phase 3.

## 10. Languages

We support **English** and **हिन्दी (Hindi)** today. Toggle from the top-right **EN / हिं** button — the entire app and notifications switch instantly. More Indian languages are on the roadmap.

## 11. Privacy

VFast follows India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023**:
- We only collect what's needed to deliver (name, phone, address, order history).
- You can request access, correction, or erasure by writing to our **Data Protection Officer** at `dpo@vmart.local`.
- Data is stored in India.

The full privacy notice is linked from the footer.
